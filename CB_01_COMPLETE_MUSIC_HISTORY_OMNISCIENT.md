## üéº FUNDAMENTALS

### Pitch & Frequency
- A4 = 440 Hz (standard tuning)
- Octave = frequency doubling
- 12 semitones per octave (Western)
- Cents: 100 cents = 1 semitone

### Rhythm
- Beat, tempo, meter
- Simple meters: 2/4, 3/4, 4/4
- Compound meters: 6/8, 9/8, 12/8
- Complex meters: 5/4, 7/8, 11/8
- Polyrhythm, polymeters
- Swing, shuffle, groove

### Scales & Modes

**Major Scale**: W-W-H-W-W-W-H
**Natural Minor**: W-H-W-W-H-W-W
**Harmonic Minor**: Raised 7th
**Melodic Minor**: Raised 6th & 7th ascending

**Church Modes**:
1. Ionian (Major)
2. Dorian (minor with raised 6th)
3. Phrygian (minor with flat 2nd)
4. Lydian (major with raised 4th)
5. Mixolydian (major with flat 7th)
6. Aeolian (Natural minor)
7. Locrian (diminished)

**Other Scales**:
- Pentatonic (major/minor)
- Blues scale
- Whole tone
- Chromatic
- Diminished (whole-half, half-whole)
- Bebop scales
- Exotic: Hungarian, Persian, Japanese, etc.

### Intervals
- Unison, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, Octave
- Major, Minor, Perfect, Augmented, Diminished
- Consonance vs Dissonance

### Chords

**Triads**:
- Major: 1-3-5
- Minor: 1-b3-5
- Diminished: 1-b3-b5
- Augmented: 1-3-#5

**Seventh Chords**:
- Major 7: 1-3-5-7
- Dominant 7: 1-3-5-b7
- Minor 7: 1-b3-5-b7
- Half-diminished: 1-b3-b5-b7
- Diminished 7: 1-b3-b5-bb7
- Minor-major 7: 1-b3-5-7

**Extended Chords**:
- 9ths, 11ths, 13ths
- Add chords (add9, add11)
- Sus chords (sus2, sus4)
- Altered chords

### Chord Progressions

**Common Progressions**:
- I-IV-V-I (Blues, Rock)

- I-vi-IV-V (50s progression)
- vi-IV-I-V (Modern pop)
- I-IV-vi-V
- Circle of fifths

**Jazz Progressions**:
- ii-V-I (most common)

### Harmony

**Voice Leading**:
- Smooth motion between chords
