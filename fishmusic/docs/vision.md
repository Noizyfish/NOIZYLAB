# Vision

> Fish Music: Structured creativity for music production

## Mission

Bring engineering discipline to music production without killing the vibe.

## Principles

1. **Track everything** - Sessions, stems, versions
2. **Name consistently** - Machine-readable, human-friendly
3. **Automate repetition** - Scripts over manual work
4. **Preserve context** - Why decisions were made

## Goals

- Zero lost work
- Instant project recall
- Reproducible mixes
- Efficient collaboration

## Non-Goals

- Restricting creativity
- Over-engineering simple tasks
- Replacing DAW workflows
